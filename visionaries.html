Chairman



Shri Banwarilalji Purohit is the guiding force of Shri Ramdeobaba college of Engineering and Management right from its establishment in 1984. Under his leadership RCOEM has been registering progress in different domains of academics, research and co-curricular activities.

Shri Banwarilal Purohit served as Minister of State for Urban Development, Slum Improvement and Housing. He was also elected to the Parliament twice, in 1984 and 1996. Shri Purohit is well- known social worker. His services in the field social welfare, promotion of womenâ€™s education, Indian culture, sports, religion and spirituality are note-worthy. He is managing Editor of the English leading daily The Hitavada from Nagpur. Besides being the Chairman of Nagpur unit of Bharatiya Vidya Bhavan, he has the honour of being appointed as a National trustee of Bharatiya Vidya Bhavan chain of schools having more than 100 branches in India and abroad.



General Secretary



Shri Govindlalji Agarwal who is a well known industrialist of central India is associated with the college since 1984. He is contributing the institution in the capacity of General Secretary. His keen interest in education and his commitment to work has enabled the institute to achieve glory and reputation. He plays a significant role in the matters of formulation and implementation of policies for the college. He is actively associated with wide range of social and religious organisations.
