Welcome To RCOEM



RCOEM was established in 1984 by Shri Ramdeobaba Sarvajanik Samiti (SRSS), a trust which has been involved in community service for over four decades. 31 years of existence has helped RCOEM grow deep roots and establish a strong foundation in technical education. Journey of a student in this institute has always involved comprehensive knowledge building from practical skills, theoretical knowledge to personality development, which has given them a head-start in their career.
